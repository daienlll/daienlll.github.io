About Me

Antoine Michels

I was born and raised in the DFW area. At 19 I went to work for FedEx as a package handler on the night shift. After 4.5 years in this position, I resigned and went to work for a logistics company named Saddlecreek. Saddlecreek provided logistical services for anumber of different companies, selling anumber of different products. The customer I worked for was CatchCo. CatchCo sells a wide variety of fishing equipment. After 1.5 years with Saddlecreek, I recieved the opportunity to work for a friend of mine at Texas Recovery Systems. Texas Recovery Systems is an electronics recycling company. While working for Texas Recovery Systems, my mother encouraged me to persue higher education. My friend had recomended SMU's bootcamp to me and the rest is history.
